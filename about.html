hey, I’m Joanna!
I’m a current sophomore at USC’s Iovine and Young Academy studying the intersection of design, technology, and business.
Growing up, I was the kid that was always drawing on my homework. Whether it was my worksheets or my piano sheet music, you could always find small doodles as a mark of what’s mine.

I soon found a love for technology as another avenue of creation, and I was constantly looking for opportunities to combine my interests. This was how I stumbled upon UI/UX.

Now as an IYA student, I’ve channeled that creative energy into learning design. In addition to design, I’ve been able to learn motion graphics, design strategy, end-to-end web development, and entrepreneurship through my interdisciplinary education. Most of all, I’ve learned how to create and to embrace being maker.

Outside of my creative work, I love cafe hopping, cute cat mobile games, and try new LA food spots!
